Biryani

Description

One of the most royal delicacies that you can enjoy on any occasion or festival, Chicken Biryani is the epitome of a one-pot meal. Well, no one can resist the sight of the aromatic and delicious Chicken Biryani recipe. If you are also craving that, then you need not go anywhere as we have got this super-easy biryani recipe for you.

Ingredients

  • 600 gm chicken
  • 1 tablespoon garam masala powder
  • 1 teaspoon saffron
  • 1 tablespoon bay leaf
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4 green chillies
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste

Steps:

  1. Turn the flame to medium again and add garam masala in it along with ginger julienned, coriander and mint leaves.
  2. Add kewra water, rose water and saffron water in it. Cook till the chicken is tender.
  3. Then add 1 cup cooked rice and spread evenly.
  4. Then add saffron water and pour ghee over it.
  5. You can now cook the dish without the lid or cover it with a lid to give a dum-effect due to the steam formation
