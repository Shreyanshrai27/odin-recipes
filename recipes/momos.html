Momos

Description

Momos are a popular street food in northern parts of India. These are also known as Dim Sum and are basically dumplings made from flour with a savory stuffing.

Momos can be steamed, baked or deep-fried. We prefer steamed momos over the fried ones. This recipe is for making steamed momos.

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • salt
  • oil
  • ginger garlic paste
  • veggies
  • chilli and soya sauce
  • vinegar

Steps:

  1. Finely cut all vegetables
  2. Add all purpose flour and oil and water and salt and make a dough
  3. saute all the chopped veggies in oil and add al the sauces mentioned in the description
  4. cut a small portion from the dough and roll it and fill the sauted veggies and give whatever sape you like
  5. steam the momos and they are ready to eat
